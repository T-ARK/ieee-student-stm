About IEEE

The world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology.

Connecting the World of Engineering

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. With a global community of over 400,000 members in more than 160 countries, IEEE inspires a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.

Vision

IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.


Mission

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

About Our Student Branch

Est. December 2023 | Code: STB99346

Fostering Innovation at STM

The IEEE Student Branch at St. Thomas College of Engineering and Technology (IEEE SB STM) is a vibrant student-led community established in 2023. Under the guidance of our Branch Counsellor, Dr. Sreekesh Namboodiri T, we strive to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry standards.

Our purpose is to create a platform where students can explore technology, develop leadership skills, and build a strong professional network. We encourage curiosity, collaboration, and responsible innovation.
